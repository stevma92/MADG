Types of Searches

Police conduct two different stop and searches, frisk searches and strip searches.

Frisk searches:

  • Police search your clothing or pockets
  • Police frisk your body for illegal or dangerous items you may be concealing.

Strip Searches

  • Police require you to remove clothes to find potential illegal items or substances.
  • Police must not touch any private areas of the body
  • The officer conducting the search must be the same sex as the suspect.
