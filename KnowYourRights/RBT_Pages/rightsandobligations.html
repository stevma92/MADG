Rights & Obligations

Some rights during an RBT stop include:

  • Rights to refuse an RBT if you are unable to complete a breath test for medical reasons
  • The right to be informed how the RBT works and what it is
  • The right not to be held by police longer than necessary to conduct an RBT

During an RBT your obligations include:

  • Providing your name, address and drivers' license when stopped by police
  • Completing the breathalyser test (unless for some reason, you are unable to)
  • Cooperating with police, especially if found guilty of drink driving
