Procedure

In WA, police may pull you over randomly or if they suspect you have committed an offence, and you will then be subject to an RBT. The RBT process is as follows:

  • Police will administer you with a breathalyser machine for you to blow into(BAC)
  • The device will produce a Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC) reading
  • If you fall within legal limits, police will let you go
  • If you fail the RBT, you are under arrest for drink driving and will be taken to a police station for secondary breath analysis
