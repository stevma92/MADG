Rights

During interactions with police, community members have a series of rights that can protect them and aid the situation. Some of the rights available to you include:

  • The right to be treated with respect, fairness and honesty from police
  • Requesting police officers to identify themselves
  • Having the ability to contact family, friends or legal representatives if you are detained
  • Having police inform you of any charges imposed on you
  • Maintaining your safety and well-being once detained by police
  • Being detained for as long as lawfully necessary
