Rights & Obligations

Sometimes during police interactions, you have the right to silence and to not disclose certain information to police. Rights concerning police stops include:

  • The right to silence if police question you about suspected crime you may have committed or contributed to
  • You have the right to legal representatives if police want to question you about said suspected crimes.
