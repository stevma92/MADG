The  Criminal  Investigation  Act  2006
(WA)  outlines  all  the laws  regarding
stop  and  search  procedures  by police.

This  act  explains  the  procedure  in
detail, requirements  of  police  during
searches and types of searches.

Additionally, this  act outlines searches
with and  without  warrants, as  well  as
procedures during  arrest  and seizure of
items.
